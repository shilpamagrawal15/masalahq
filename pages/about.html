About Us

There is a whole desi (or subtly desi) cultural scene out there in your city and MasalaHQ will help you discover that. Our goal is that you don't miss a single desi influenced cultural beat in the city. We pull together all such events in one place. Not just established artists and art forms, but also fresh faces and ideas inspired by South Asian sensibilities. Be it an acclaimed or upcoming play, a melodious Sufi musical evening, a Bollywood dance party, a South Asian film festival, an exotic museum exhibition, or a scholarly talk - MasalaHQ will keep you updated on it all. So go ahead, and find something you enjoy! Also, don't miss the outstanding events that our hard working staff selects every week in Masala Picks.
